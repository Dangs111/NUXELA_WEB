NUXELA's Own App

We're developing our own nutrition app to validate our marketing strategies and showcase the power of our content tools.

The app is in early development stage, not the final version

Why a Mobile App?

We anticipate significant growth in the app and SaaS sector, particularly as AI enables creators to implement their ideas more effectively. This market segment is our primary focus as it aligns perfectly with our content marketing tools and expertise.

  • Food and health content is consistently popular on social media, making it ideal for content marketing
  • We're developing using a marketing-first approach to build more effective campaigns
  • Real-world testing validates our tools while building our own user base

Our Niche: Vitamin & Micronutrient Tracking

While the food tracking app market is competitive, we're specializing in vitamin and micronutrient tracking based on growing interest in dietary supplements and understanding how different elements in foods contribute to optimal body functioning.
